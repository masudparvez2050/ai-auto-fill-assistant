# AI Auto Fill Assistant - Privacy Policy

## Last Updated: May 23, 2025

## Overview

AI Auto Fill Assistant is a Chrome extension that helps users fill out forms and enhance text content using AI technology. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

### User-Provided Information

- **API Keys**: If you choose to use your own AI provider (Gemini, OpenAI, or Claude), you will need to provide your API key. This is stored locally in your browser's storage and is never transmitted to our servers.
- **Form Content**: When you use the extension to generate or enhance content, the text in your form fields is temporarily processed to create appropriate AI responses.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the extension, such as which features are used most frequently.

## How We Use Information

- **To Provide Services**: We use form context and content to generate appropriate AI responses for form fields.
- **To Improve Our Extension**: We analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality and user experience.

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your API keys and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.sync API.
- **Server Processing**: When generating content with AI, data is sent directly from your browser to the respective AI provider's API (Google, OpenAI, or Anthropic) using your provided API key. We do not store or process this data on our servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The only data sharing occurs when content is sent to the AI provider you've selected (using your own API key) to generate responses.

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information:

- API keys are encrypted in local storage
- All API communications use HTTPS
- No sensitive data is stored on our servers

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access, update or delete your information
- Opt-out of any future communications from us
- Request information about how your data is used

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@codevionix.com.
